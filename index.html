Permanent Makeup Specialist

These Are Our Services



Microblading &
Powder Eyebrow

Microblading is a semi-permanent makeup procedure. Hair like incision strokes are created along the eyebrow to enhance, reshape, cover gaps of lost hair, extend eyebrows, or fully reconstruct eyebrows with little to no hair.











Permanent Eyeliner
& Eyelash Enhancement

Permanent Eyeliner, also known as Lash Enhancement, gives you perfectly applied eyeliner that highlights and enhances the natural beauty of your eye shape. Say goodbye to drawing on your eyeliner every morning! Your eyelashes will appear thicker, even when mascara is not worn.

Color is applied along your lash line. This can be applied to the top, bottom, or both top and bottom during your appointment. Any desired look can be achieved. It is also referred to as Lash Enhancement. Treatment takes 90 minutes and lasts up to 2 years.



Permanent Lip Color
& Lip Blushing

Permanent Lip Color is the truly smudge-proof answer to all day, perfect lip color. Say goodbye to touching up your lip color every day! Using our special device, color is applied to enhance your lip line and/or full lip in any shade you choose. This will naturally blush the lips, making them appear more youthful. Lip shading can be applied to look like a lipstick, lip tint, or the softest lip blush. Lip lines can be adjusted to create more fullness and symmetry.

Treatment takes about 120 minutes and lasts up to 3 years.



Skin Tag Removal

An electric needle removes a wide variety of common and minor skin irregularities such as skin tags, blood spots, raised freckles and cholesterol deposits. The removed areas are cauterized so there is no bleeding. The skin forms a small scab which falls off in about 1 week. The pigment eventually fades and leaves no sign of the treatment. The pigment eventually fades and immediate results in about 3 - 5 seconds. This treatment is non-invasive and effectively treats conditions with little discomfort.
