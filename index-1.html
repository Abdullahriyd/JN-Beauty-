Face and Skin Care

These Are Our Services

Eyebrows: Microblading

Microblading is a semi-permanent makeup procedure. Hair like incision strokes are created along the eyebrow to enhance, reshape, cover gaps of lost hair, extend eyebrows, or fully reconstruct eyebrows with little to no hair.

Shading

Microshading uses pin-like dots of pigmentation for a powdered eyebrow look. Shading is versatile and can be performed alone or with microblading for a subtle or bold brow. Shading lasts a little longer than blading.

Ombré

Ombré shading is the newest trend in eyebrow semi-permanent makeup. It gives a soft shaded brow pencil look. Once healed, it is more natural looking than solid color. It gives great definition and depth to the brows.

Combo Brow

The combination of both microblading and shading gives hair-like texture, definition and density. Sometimes microblading alone can look too light. By adding shading, you get the density as well as hair strokes from microblading.

Lip Blushing

Lip blushing is a semipermanent form of cosmetic tattooing which deposits pigment in your lips using small needles. Sometimes called lip tattooing, this is a cosmetic enhancement altering both color and shape to create younger-looking lips.

Eyeliner Enhancement

Eyeliner is a type of permanent makeup (cosmetic tattoo). This technique gives your top lashes a fuller, darker effect while outlining and framing your eye. This eyeliner technique sits directly on the top lash line and blends into your lashes.

Skin Tag Removal

Skin tag removal can be done with immediate results. Cryotherapy freezes off the skin tag using liquid nitrogen. Another option is electrocautery, in which an electric probe or needle is used to burn off the skin tag.
